# Knight Dash

A 2D pixel-style fighting game where two knights battle on a single screen.

## Features
- **2-Player Local Duel**: Battle it out with a friend on the same screen.
- **Maps**: 10 unique maps to fight on.
- **Combat**: Best of 5 rounds format.

## How to Play
1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Use the keyboard to control your knight and defeat your opponent!
